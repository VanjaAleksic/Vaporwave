DEFINITION

Vaporwave is a microgenre of electronic music and an Internet meme that emerged in the early 2010s.

MUSIC

Style is defined by its borrowing of 1980s and 1990s mood music styles such as smooth jazz, elevator music, R&B, and lounge music.

CULTURE

Characterized by a nostalgic or surrealist engagement with the popular entertainment, technology and advertising of previous decades.
